About Us

We are a full service auto maintenance and repair center based in Donlholm. We specialize in vehicle maintenance service, electrical service, transmission services, heavy duty services and much more.

We have over 10 years of experience and the knowledge to service and repair even the most challenging automotive problems on all makes and models. We use the latest technology to assess the situation and offer you solutions or alternatives.

We have built our business on the principles of honesty, integrity and hard work to maintain a staff who reflect those same ideals. Everything we do in our facility is aimed at establishing a trust based relationship with our clientele that will last a lifetime.

We take part in regular on-going training to ensure that we are always up to date with the current auto repair technology. We are also using the latest diagnostic equipment available. This ensures a proper diagnosis the first time you bring in your car, saving you considerable time and costs.

We also ensure the privacy of your personal information that we come into contact with, safety, security and safely return of valuables and documents.

Our Services

01

Engine Diagnostic

02

Electricals and Electronics

We help improve your visibility by inspecting the light, battery and charging system check, central locking test, power windows, multi-media test and many more.

03

Auto Body Repair

We provide proffessional grade detailing and upgrades. We replace damaged bady parts, major structural repairs such as aligning the body, removing dents, buffing, painting and also applying new finish to restored body parts.

Wheel Alignment

We make sure that all 4 wheels point in the same direction, which will cause the vehicle to drive straight and not drift. This ensures safe and accurate steering, as well as directional changes. Regulary doing this extends the life of your tires.

05

Does anything rattle when you drive over bumps or potholes? All the moving parts of your suspension affect everything from tire wear to ride comfort, steering response to handling, and much more. We service shocks, ball-joints, streering racks and much more.

Full Vehicle Service
